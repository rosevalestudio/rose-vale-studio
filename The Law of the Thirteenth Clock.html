The Law of the Thirteenth Clock

A fantasy mystery about four friends, an unfinished Black Book, and a world running out of time.

When Shinichi, Eunoia, Zea, and Zelen discover a strange Black Book hidden inside Rosewood Athenaeum, they make one irreversible choice: they say “Begin.”

Bound to the Book by matching spider lily marks, the four must recover its 288 missing pages, uncover ancient Keys, survive fractured time, and learn why the hidden world of Chronobell appears to recognize them.
