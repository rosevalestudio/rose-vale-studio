Building original stories from the ground up

Rose Vale Studio is a creator-owned studio focused on developing original novels, fantasy worlds, character art, stationery, collectibles, and digital products.

What Rose Vale Studio is

Rose Vale Studio is being built as an independent creative company centered on original intellectual property. The studio develops stories first, then expands those stories through artwork, worldbuilding, physical products, and digital releases.

The studio is currently in its development stage. That means the main work right now includes writing, visual design, website development, product planning, research, and building a complete library of original creative assets.

The goal is not to create random merchandise around unrelated ideas. Every product should connect directly to a story, character, location, creature, artifact, or piece of lore from a Rose Vale Studio world.

The Law of the Thirteenth Clock

The Law of the Thirteenth Clock is the first major original project being developed under Rose Vale Studio. It is a fantasy mystery centered on four lifelong friends, a mysterious Black Book, missing pages, supernatural artifacts, fractured time, and a hidden world divided into six Domains.

The novel is being developed alongside its visual world. This includes character designs, creatures, Keys, potions, materials, crystals, maps, architecture, currencies, locations, and future merchandise concepts.

Long-term vision

Rose Vale Studio is intended to grow into an independent creative company known for immersive original worlds, strong visual identity, and merchandise that meaningfully connects to its stories.

The studio will begin with books and a carefully selected product catalog. As more artwork, stories, and resources are completed, the catalog can expand into larger collectibles, lore publications, audiobooks, and other creative formats.
