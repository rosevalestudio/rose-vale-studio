Game Concept Overview

A story-driven exploration game set inside the Rose Vale universe, focused on mystery, memory, and discovery through interactive worldbuilding.

Core Concept

The game follows a narrative exploration structure where the player uncovers fragmented lore, interacts with symbolic environments, and slowly reconstructs the hidden history of Rose Vale.
